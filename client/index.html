Explore Our Features

"Basic Features are included with three new features that will help the students in their studies."

Settings

Settings will allow you to be able to change your profile picture, password, and other settings, it also include dark theme and white theme settings.

Study Group

Study Group is a feature that allows you to create a group with your classmates and discuss about your studies, share your notes and ask questions.

Calendar Note Taking

Calendar Note Taking is a feature that allows you to take notes and save them in the calendar, you can also set a reminder for your notes.

Creating Courses

Creating Courses is a feature that allows you to create a course and share it with your classmates, you can also add a quiz and assignment to your course.

Upload Content

Upload Content is a feature that allows you to upload your notes, assignment, and other study materials, you can also share it with your classmates.

Chatting With Classmate

Chatting With Classmate is a feature that allows you to chat with your classmates, you can also create a group chat with your classmates.

LMS is a platform where you can learn with each other and share your knowledge with one another without any cost.
